About Me

"Study the science of art. Study the art of science. Develope your senses - especially learn how to see." - Lenoardo da Vinci



Hello, I'm Charlotte. A cross-functional professional with a constructive attitude and a penchant for always learning something new. I enjoy turning complex problems into simple, beautiful and intuitive applications. I enjoy exploring new libraries,frameworks, and getting front and back ends to play nicely.

After 10 years as a scientist, I found my LOVE for coding after spending countless nights of data-handling and working with our department's horribly designed and "clunky" database tools. I wrote my own scripts using VBA to ease my frustrations as well as created my own custom run-time protocols for liquid handling robotic systems. My early coding adventures, lead to an increase in productivity and was eventually used by my whole department for high-thoroughput screening projects. *pats own back*

(Click here to check out my publications if you don't believe me.)

I decided to follow my instincs and joined a fullstack development bootcamp where I have indulged in my new found love and learned HTML,CSS, and javascript.

My main goal is to LEARN MORE languages, gain new skills, and create applications that can assist people in all walks of life.

It's my new calling...
